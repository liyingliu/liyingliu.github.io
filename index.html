Hi, I am Liying, graduated from CentraleSupélec in France with a diplôme d’Ingénieur (M. Eng) in Applied Mathematics. I like to use AI to solve problems in real life. My research interests lie on deep learning, computer vision, probabilistic reasoning, reinforcement learning and other latest trends in AI.

I am currently a research engineer in Hanalytics, mainly working on semantic segmentation and classification on medical images.
Before that, I was a research intern in Krakotoa, a leading innovator of seismic attribute analysis and reservoir modeling software and services based on AI, working on Deep Reinforcement Learning; semi-supervised learning for reservoir modeling/geobody prediction on seismic data.
In 2016, I was a research intern in the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at National University of Singapore, working on building deep learning models for early glaucoma detection on 3D Optical Coherence Tomography images.
